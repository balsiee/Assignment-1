Monkey D. Luffy - Future King of Pirates

Monkey D. Luffy is from the East Blue Sea.

19 Years of age.

He ate the Gum Gum Devil Fruit when he was 11 years of age.

His entire body is comprised of a rubber like material giving him the ability to stretch his limbs and any part of his body to his liking.

Monkey D. Luffy is the son of Monkey D. Dragon, the grandson of Monkey D. Garp, and the adoptive younger brother of the late Portgas D. Ace and the revolutionary Sabo.

He is the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates (named for the straw hat Shanks had given him the last time they met), as well as one of it's four strongest members alongside Roronoa Zoro, Sanji, and Jinbe. The World Government views Luffy as a major threat, due to him directly targeting them and their operations multiple times and for being the son of the Revolutionary Dragon.

Most importanly Monkey D. Luffy cares about his friends more than anything and will go to any lengths to keep them from harm.
